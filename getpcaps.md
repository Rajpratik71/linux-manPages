NAME
       get_pcaps - List Process Capabilities

SYNOPSIS
       get_pcaps <pid> [<pid> ...]

DESCRIPTION
       get_pcaps is a simple utility to display the capabilities on the queried process(es).  The capabilities are displayed in the cap_from_text(3) format.
